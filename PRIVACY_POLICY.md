# Privacy Policy

Welcome to Habibi Swipe ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application (the "App"). **Profile Information:**

**Payment Information:**

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected

**Usage Information:**

**Location Information:**

**Technical Information:**

**App Stores:**
